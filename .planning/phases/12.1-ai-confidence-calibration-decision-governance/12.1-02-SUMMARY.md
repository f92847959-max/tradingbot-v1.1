# Phase 12.1 Plan 02 Summary: Runtime Decision Governance

Implemented the runtime governance layer for calibrated ensemble decisions.

- Replaced the old ad hoc final confidence/conflict hard gates with artifact-driven governance decisions.
- Added regime propagation, threshold source reporting, gate decisions, and audit details to the final aggregation payload.
