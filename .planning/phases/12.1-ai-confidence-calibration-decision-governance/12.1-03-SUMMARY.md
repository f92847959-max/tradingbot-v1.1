# Phase 12.1 Plan 03 Summary: Governance Persistence and Challenger Monitoring

Implemented the operational layer for calibrated governance decisions.

One-liner: governance outcomes are now persisted as first-class audit rows, the trading loop stores HOLD/rejected/pass decisions with sanitized artifact versions, and calibrated champion/challenger plus retraining decisions are expressed through explicit helper functions and monitoring status.

## What Changed

- Added `GovernanceDecision` ORM storage plus `GovernanceDecisionRepository` for recent-query and audit-safe persistence patterns.
- Extended `trading_loop.py` so governance decisions are written even when the AI returns HOLD, StrategyManager filters the signal, or trade execution is blocked later.
- Added `promotion.py` with calibrated challenger-promotion and retraining-trigger evaluation based on Brier/log-loss, drawdown, profit factor, and trade-count evidence.
- Expanded `ModelMonitor` from raw-confidence checks to calibrated degradation status, including Brier score, drawdown, profit factor, and retraining recommendations.
- Added focused tests for audit persistence, recent decision retrieval, artifact-version sanitization, challenger promotion gating, and calibrated retraining triggers.
