## Threat Model Seeds

| Threat Ref | Threat | Required Mitigation |
|------------|--------|---------------------|
| T-12.7-01 | Short broker history reaches model fitting and wastes compute or creates misleading artifacts | early data coverage preflight before feature/label/model stages |
| T-12.7-02 | Row loss is invisible, hiding why trainable samples collapsed | structured row-loss report with stage counts and reasons |
| T-12.7-03 | Label features leak future outcomes into training inputs | causal label builder, future-row mutation tests, and split manifest |
| T-12.7-04 | Candidate uplift is inflated by different walk-forward windows | window identity and purge-gap manifest shared by champion and candidate |
| T-12.7-05 | Promotion passes on raw accuracy while trading quality degrades | PF, drawdown, cost-adjusted metrics, and non-HOLD checks |
| T-12.7-06 | Failed candidate still updates production pointers | explicit promotion decision gate before `production.json` changes |
