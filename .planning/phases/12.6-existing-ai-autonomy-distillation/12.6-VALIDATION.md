## Threat Model Seeds

| Threat Ref | Threat | Required Mitigation |
|------------|--------|---------------------|
| T-12.6-01 | Snapshot capture happens after broker or post-filter side effects and leaks future execution state | capture immediately after raw signal generation and persist observation vs outcomes separately |
| T-12.6-02 | Blocked decisions collapse into `HOLD`, teaching the student the wrong abstain behavior | hierarchical labels with `block_stage`, `block_codes`, and explicit action masks |
| T-12.6-03 | Candidate artifacts overwrite core or specialist roots or use incompatible calibration schema | isolated artifact root plus existing calibration/versioning contracts |
| T-12.6-04 | Promotion passes on accuracy while HOLD dominates and trading quality degrades | require PF, drawdown, calibration, and non-HOLD quality gates |
| T-12.6-05 | Candidate runtime bypasses `DecisionGovernor`, `StrategyManager`, event windows, or risk checks | inject autonomy at raw-signal boundary only and keep downstream chain unchanged |
| T-12.6-06 | Pre-AI blocks or disagreement reasons are lost from audit records | persist structured rollout metadata in governance evaluation summaries |
